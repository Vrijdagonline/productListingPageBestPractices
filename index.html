An optimal usage of LazyLoad both for regular and alternative images loaded at mouseover

Features:

  1. Conditional loads LazyLoad v. 8 or 10 depending on IntersectionObserver support
  2. Eagerly loads the first 2 images
  3. Lazily loads the images from the 3rd on
  4. If the user device has a mouse:
    1. Lazily loads the alternative images which appear at mouseover
    2. Fade-in effect of the alternative images ONLY after their loading is complete
